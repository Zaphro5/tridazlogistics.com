Order Placement and Pickup Arrangement

The first stage involves the customer placing an order and providing relevant details such as pickup location, item description, and any specific instructions. Tridaz Logistics works closely with the customer to schedule and arrange the pickup, ensuring a convenient and efficient process.

Order Pickup and Transit

Once the pickup is scheduled, Tridaz Logistics dispatches a dedicated team to the specified location for order collection. Our experienced professionals handle the items with care, ensuring proper packaging and loading onto our transportation vehicles. The order then begins its transit journey, where it is safeguarded and tracked using advanced logistics technology.

Order Delivery and Confirmation

In the final stage, Tridaz Logistics ensures timely and secure delivery of the order to the designated destination. Our team follows the predetermined route and adheres to the specified delivery instructions. Upon successful delivery, the recipient confirms receipt, and Tridaz Logistics updates the customer with proof of delivery and any relevant documentation.

Our Services

Order Pickup and Delivery

We specialize in the seamless pickup of orders from your designated location and ensuring their safe and timely transportation to the desired destinations within the Philippines. Our dedicated team and reliable network of vehicles are ready to handle orders of all sizes, ensuring efficient and secure delivery.

Last-Mile Delivery

Our last-mile delivery service ensures that your orders reach their final destinations swiftly and accurately. With our optimized route planning and real-time tracking, we guarantee reliable and on-time delivery, enhancing your customer experience and satisfaction.

Express Delivery

When time is of the essence, our express delivery service provides expedited transportation for urgent orders. We prioritize these shipments, utilizing our efficient logistics network to ensure they are picked up promptly and delivered with the utmost speed and reliability.

Same-Day Delivery

For those time-sensitive orders, our same-day delivery service ensures that they are picked up and transported to their destinations within the same day. With our focus on speed and efficiency, we meet tight deadlines while maintaining the integrity and security of your orders.

Scheduled Pickup and Delivery

We understand that some businesses require regular and scheduled transportation services. With our scheduled pickup and delivery service, you can rely on us to pick up orders at predetermined times and transport them to their destinations according to your specified schedule, providing convenience and peace of mind.

Special Handling and Fragile Goods

Certain orders require special care and handling, especially when it comes to fragile or sensitive items. We have the expertise and experience to handle such goods with utmost caution, employing proper packaging, protective measures, and specialized handling to ensure their safe and secure transportation.
