Privacy Policy

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, delivery addresses, and payment information when you engage with Tridaz Logistic Services. This information is collected through various channels, including our website, customer support interactions, and order processing.

The personal information we collect is used to provide and improve our services, process your orders, communicate with you, and address any inquiries or concerns. We may also use your information for administrative purposes, data analysis, and to comply with legal obligations.

Tridaz Logistic Services may share your personal information with trusted third parties who assist us in delivering our services, such as shipping partners or payment processors. We ensure that these parties adhere to strict confidentiality and security measures. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. We use secure technologies, encryption, and robust data management practices to safeguard your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize content, analyze website traffic, and collect information about your interactions with our site. You have the option to disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that this may affect certain functionalities of our website.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. Tridaz Logistic Services is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those third parties before providing any personal information.

Tridaz Logistic Services operates in compliance with applicable laws and regulations governing data protection and privacy. We may disclose personal information when required by law or in response to valid legal requests from government authorities.

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
